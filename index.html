A different kind of therapy

Is faith-based therapy right for you?

Sometimes life presents seemingly insurmountable difficulties, and it can feel dark and lonely. You don’t have to choose between sound clinical care and your faith — here, they work together.

Faith, fully integrated

As a Christian therapist, I draw on biblical principles to address spiritual needs alongside proven, evidence-based methods — never one instead of the other.

Led entirely by you

Your goals set the direction. Scripture and prayer are woven in only if and when you want them. People of all backgrounds are warmly welcome.

Experienced and grounded

With more than two decades treating trauma, addiction, and life’s deepest wounds, I’ll partner with you to find clarity in the midst of your storm.

A compassionate space

Detransition & transition-regret support

If you are navigating detransition or transition regret, you deserve a therapist who understands — without judgment and without an agenda. As someone who has walked this road myself, I offer voluntary, client-led, trauma-informed support from a Christian perspective.

This is supportive talk therapy that follows your goals. It is never coercive, and it is not conversion therapy.

Getting started is simple

What to expect

  1. 1

    Reach out

    Send a message or email. We’ll find a time for a brief, no-pressure conversation about what’s bringing you in.

  2. 2

    In your first session we’ll talk through your goals at your pace. Faith is invited only if and when you want it.
